Crab Creek Crawl

Dive into the Chesapeake Bay’s rich crab history and cuisine.

Overview

Join the Crab Creek Crawl for a tour centered around Maryland's most famous culinary export: the blue crab. Experience crab in every form, from steamed to cakes, while exploring the Chesapeake Bay region.

What's Included

  • Crab tastings at 3 different locations
  • Crab cake preparation demonstration
  • Guided tour along the Chesapeake Bay

Tour Itinerary

Stop 1: Enjoy a steamed crab feast at a local seafood shack.

Stop 2: Watch a crab cake preparation demo, followed by a tasting.

Stop 3: Relax along the Chesapeake Bay with crab bisque and a bay view.
