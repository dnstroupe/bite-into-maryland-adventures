Harbor Hush Puppy Hop

Crispy seafood bites by the Chesapeake Bay with stunning views.

Overview

The Harbor Hush Puppy Hop combines incredible views of the Chesapeake Bay with Maryland’s best battered seafood. From hush puppies to crispy crab balls, this tour will have you indulging in fried favorites.

What's Included

  • Hush puppies, crab balls, and other fried seafood tastings
  • Bayfront seating with stunning views of the Chesapeake
  • Drink pairing options with local craft beer

Tour Itinerary

Stop 1: Start with hush puppies and craft beer at a waterfront eatery.

Stop 2: Taste crispy crab balls at a famous seafood spot by the bay.

Stop 3: End with a fried seafood platter, including shrimp, fish, and more.
