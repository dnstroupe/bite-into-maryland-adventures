Annapolis Appetites

Savor history and flavors in Maryland's capital.

Overview

Explore the rich history and flavors of Annapolis, Maryland. From fresh seafood to classic Maryland cuisine, this tour will take you through historic landmarks and introduce you to the best local dishes Annapolis has to offer.

What's Included

  • 5+ tastings from historic Annapolis restaurants
  • Guided tour of the Annapolis State House and historic district
  • Sample classic Maryland dishes such as oysters and soft-shell crabs

Tour Itinerary

Stop 1: Begin at the Annapolis State House, the oldest U.S. state capitol in continuous legislative use.

Stop 2: Enjoy Maryland's famed oysters at a waterside restaurant.

Stop 3: Visit a historic tavern for traditional Chesapeake Bay cuisine.

Stop 4: Savor soft-shell crabs, a local delicacy, paired with seasonal vegetables.

Stop 5: End the tour with a sweet treat at a renowned Annapolis bakery.
