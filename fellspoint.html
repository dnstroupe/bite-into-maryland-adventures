Fells Point Flavor Frenzy

A bite-sized Baltimore bliss.

Overview

Discover the historic Fells Point while indulging in delicious local flavors. This tour takes you through some of the best local spots, offering an array of Baltimore’s iconic dishes, including crab cakes, oysters, and more.

What's Included

  • 6+ food tastings from Fells Point’s top spots
  • Visit historical landmarks along the waterfront
  • Local guide sharing fun facts and stories
  • Tastings include crab cakes, oysters, local beers, and more

Tour Itinerary

Stop 1: Start your journey at the Fells Point Waterfront.

Stop 2: Enjoy a classic Maryland crab cake at a local seafood joint.

Stop 3: Savor fresh oysters while learning their local significance.

Stop 4: Stop by a craft brewery for a taste of local beers.

Stop 5: Finish with dessert from a famous Baltimore bakery.
