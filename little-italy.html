Little Italy Lunar Bites

Savor the Italian flavors of Baltimore’s historic Little Italy.

Overview

Journey through Little Italy to experience authentic Italian dishes and a rich culinary heritage. Enjoy traditional recipes with a modern twist.

What's Included

  • Tastings of authentic Italian pasta, cured meats, and desserts
  • Explore Baltimore’s historic Little Italy with a local guide
  • Visit iconic Italian restaurants and cafes

Tour Itinerary

Stop 1: Start with fresh bruschetta and Italian bread at a local café.

Stop 2: Indulge in homemade pasta and prosciutto at an authentic trattoria.

Stop 3: End with classic tiramisu and an espresso at a cozy bakery.
