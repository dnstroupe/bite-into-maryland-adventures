Federal Hill Foodie Frontier

An adventurous journey through Baltimore’s finest and boldest bites.

Overview

Federal Hill Foodie Frontier is designed for those who love to explore bold flavors. From innovative fusion dishes to traditional Maryland favorites, this tour will push your taste buds to the frontier of culinary delight.

What's Included

  • Samples of fusion dishes combining local and international flavors
  • Drinks from local breweries and distilleries
  • Guided culinary history of Baltimore’s Federal Hill neighborhood

Tour Itinerary

Stop 1: Start with sushi rolls with a Maryland twist at a fusion bistro.

Stop 2: Taste innovative crab dumplings with a side of local craft beer.

Stop 3: End the tour with a bold dessert, mixing Chesapeake Bay flavors with international cuisine.
